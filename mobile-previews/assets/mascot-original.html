Project K — Meet your hero

Sticking with the human knight metaphor — readable, classic, and the RPG vocabulary (quest, boss, XP) lands instantly. Two playable archetypes at onboarding: Knight (heavy armor, sword & shield) and Queen (regal cloak, crown, rapier). Same sprite skeleton, same evolution stages, same XP economy — different silhouette and palette.

Both are gender-neutral as roles, not as bodies — pick whichever silhouette you vibe with. The user names theirs at onboarding.

The hero system, in one minute:

· Two archetypes at onboarding: Knight (silver/red, sword & shield) or Queen (violet/gold, rapier & orb). Pick once; can be reskinned later.

· Same five-stage evolution: Squire→Footman→Knight→Champion→King · Page→Lady→Queen→High Queen→Empress. Same level thresholds, same XP curve.

· Cosmetic palette swaps as rewards — earn dyes (crimson, jade, frost, obsidian, gold) by hitting PRs and streak milestones. Same sprite, new colors.

· Behavior maps to training. Workouts level them up, rest days they recover, skip a week and they look weary.
